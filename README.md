# Super Store Sales Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes **sales performance for a retail superstore** across regions, product categories, and individual products.  
The objective is to deliver an **executive-ready Power BI dashboard** that enables stakeholders to quickly understand business performance, profitability, and sales trends for informed decision-making.

---

## Business Problem
Retail organizations need clear visibility into:
- Which products and regions generate the most revenue
- Where profits are increasing or declining
- How sales performance changes over time  

Without a centralized reporting solution, identifying opportunities and risks becomes challenging.

---

## Tools Used
- **Power BI Desktop** – Data modeling, DAX calculations, and dashboard development  
- **Power BI Service** – Report publishing and sharing  

---

##  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard focuses on the following KPIs:
- **Total Sales** – Overall revenue generated
- **Total Profit** – Net profit after costs
- **Profit Margin (%)** – Profitability efficiency
- **Sales Trend** – Performance changes over time

---

##  Key Insights
- Top-performing **products and regions** contribute the largest share of revenue and profit
- Some categories show **strong sales but lower profit margins**, indicating pricing or cost issues
- Sales trends reveal **seasonal patterns and growth opportunities**
- Underperforming areas highlight where **strategic improvements are required**

---

##  Dashboard Features
- Executive-level KPI cards for quick performance overview  
- Sales trend analysis over time  
- Product, category, and regional performance breakdowns  
- Interactive filters for dynamic data exploration  

---

##  Business Value
This dashboard helps decision-makers to:
- Monitor overall business performance at a glance  
- Identify high-impact products and regions  
- Improve profitability through data-driven insights  
- Track trends and respond proactively to changes
